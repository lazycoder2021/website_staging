M365 Updates Jan 01, 2045

Commercial Data Protection: Microsoft’s Assurance about your data protection when you use Copilot.

To commercial users of Microsoft 365 (namely users from organizations or certain schools who log onto M365 via their Entra IDs), Microsoft offers commercial data protection when they interact with Copilot.

Microsoft assures that

  • Copilot does not access any graph data in Microsoft 365.
  • This means Copilot DOES NOT HAVE access to
    • email, or document, or reports, or files, or chat, or SharePoint site, or any content that you generate in your M365 tenant – with or without the use of Copilot.

In fact, MS assures that when you interact with Copilot to get help to do a job or produce something, even the prompts that you enter is yours. These are not retained to train and enhance Copilot. The moment a session ends, or the browser is closed, the prompts are discarded

Then, you might be tempted to ask how Copilot gets better?

Microsoft constantly upgrades Copilot and underlying LLMs through the large public dataset and research, but does not use proprietary enterprise data or prompts to do this. This is the assurance they give you.

Anonymous Chat

The highlight of Commercial Data Protection guarantee is the anonymity. Copilot uses your Entra ID only to establish your identity and to check whether you are qualified for commercial data protection. Subsequently, the identity related information is removed from the chats so that the organizational information does not fall into cracks should something go wrong.

And every interaction in the chat session is encrypted in transit (TLS 1.2+) and at rest (AES-128).

Even Microsoft cannot read/ interpret your communication with Copilot. To put it in its own words, Microsoft “has no 'eyes-on' access to it”.

The only requirement

For all this to happen the user, who is interacting with Copilot, must be signed into Copilot with organization/school-assigned Entra credentials. Commercial data protection guarantee, which is tailored to meet stringent compliance and data security needs, does not apply to personal M365 subscriptions, which has its own level of data protection measures.
